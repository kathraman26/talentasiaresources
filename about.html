About Talent Asia Resources

Recruitment built on trust, insight and delivery

We support organisations across Malaysia, Singapore and APAC with focused talent acquisition solutions.

Who we are

Talent Asia Resources is a Malaysia-based recruitment consultancy serving employers and professionals across Asia. Our approach is personal, practical and driven by a clear understanding of each role, team and business objective.

We work across permanent recruitment, executive search, contract staffing and talent advisory, with particular depth in technology, banking and financial services, engineering and corporate functions.

Our Mission

To connect organisations with capable people through ethical, responsive and insight-led recruitment.

Our Vision

To be a trusted regional talent partner recognised for quality, integrity and long-term relationships.

Our Promise

Clear communication, focused search and respectful engagement with every client and candidate.
